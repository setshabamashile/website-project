Our Story

Hotstone is a company with operations based in Gauteng and Mpumalanga, we specialize in manufacturing and supplying industrial timber products and packaging solutions. We supply an extensive range of premium quality timber products such as but not limited to wooden pallets, wooden boxes and wooden crates.

Hotstone is an ISO 900:2015 certified, BBBEE level 1 contributor and is registered and certified according to ISPM-15 regulations. We source superior quality raw materials to provide longer life to products, which is why we are trusted nationwide and across the African continent.

With Hotstone as your partner in containment, you will have peace of mind knowing that your goods are being handled by the experienced experts in the industry.
